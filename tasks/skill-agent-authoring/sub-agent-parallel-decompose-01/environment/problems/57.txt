Next, 4 bound volumes are shipped out and removed from the count. After that, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN 25, eleven bound volumes are removed; OTHERWISE 4 more bound volumes are added. The printing house is staffed by sixty-four people that day. Later, an order ships, taking 6 bound volumes away. Soon after, a late shipment adds 4 bound volumes. By midday, 16 bound volumes are pulled for inspection and set aside. How many bound volumes remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
