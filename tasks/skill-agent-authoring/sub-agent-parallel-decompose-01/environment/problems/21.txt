A printing house begins the day with 28 bound volumes. First, a late shipment adds seven bound volumes. Next, 18 bound volumes are written off after a spill. After that, an order ships, taking 14 bound volumes away. The printing house is staffed by 38 people that day. Later, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN twenty-six, 13 bound volumes are removed; OTHERWISE 14 more bound volumes are added. Soon after, a late shipment adds ten bound volumes. By midday, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN forty-nine, thirteen bound volumes are removed; OTHERWISE seven more bound volumes are added. In the afternoon, a delivery brings 6 more bound volumes. How many bound volumes remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
