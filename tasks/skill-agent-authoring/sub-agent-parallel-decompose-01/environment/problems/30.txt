A bike depot begins the day with 29 rental bikes. Mid-morning, a delivery brings nine more rental bikes. At noon the count is doubled after a second shipment arrives. In the afternoon, 11 rental bikes are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 1 more rental bikes. Overnight, 8 rental bikes are found damaged and discarded.

How many rental bikes remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
