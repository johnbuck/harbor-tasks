Background: forty-five visitors toured the recycling center, unrelated to the inventory. After that, ten sorted bins arrive from a partner site. Later, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN eleven, fourteen sorted bins are removed; OTHERWISE 13 more sorted bins are added. Soon after, four sorted bins are shipped out and removed from the count. By midday, 8 sorted bins arrive from a partner site. In the afternoon, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN forty-four, thirteen sorted bins are removed; OTHERWISE 14 more sorted bins are added. How many sorted bins remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
