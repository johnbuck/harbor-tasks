A aquarium lab begins the day with 21 tagged fish. The aquarium lab is staffed by four people that day. First, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN thirty-nine, 9 tagged fish are removed; OTHERWISE ten more tagged fish are added. Next, a merger doubles the count. After that, an order ships, taking 5 tagged fish away. Later, 13 tagged fish are pulled for inspection and set aside. Soon after, 14 tagged fish are shipped out and removed from the count. The aquarium lab is staffed by 70 people that day. By midday, fifteen tagged fish are shipped out and removed from the count. In the afternoon, a late shipment adds fifteen tagged fish. How many tagged fish remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
