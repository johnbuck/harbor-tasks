A recycling center begins the shift with twenty-three sorted bins. First, a parity rule applies — if the running total is ODD at that point, 7 sorted bins are removed; if it is EVEN, 9 more sorted bins are added. Next, eight sorted bins arrive from a partner site. The recycling center is staffed by 12 people that shift. After that, the crew restocks three additional sorted bins. Later, eight sorted bins are written off after a spill. Soon after, an order ships, taking 12 sorted bins away. The recycling center is staffed by 48 people that shift. How many sorted bins remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
