A aquarium begins the day with 26 tagged fish. Mid-morning, a delivery brings six more tagged fish. At noon the count is tripled after two more shipments arrive. In the afternoon, 12 tagged fish are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an even number, the supervisor removes exactly half of them. Overnight, 6 tagged fish are found damaged and discarded.

How many tagged fish remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
