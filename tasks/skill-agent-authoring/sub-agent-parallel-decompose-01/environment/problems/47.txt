A toy factory begins the day with 25 wooden trains. Mid-morning, a delivery brings twelve more wooden trains. At noon the count is tripled after two more shipments arrive. In the afternoon, 18 wooden trains are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 5 more wooden trains. Overnight, 3 wooden trains are found damaged and discarded.

How many wooden trains remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
