A textile mill begins the day with 41 fabric bolts. Mid-morning, a delivery brings three more fabric bolts. At noon the count is doubled after a second shipment arrives. In the afternoon, 9 fabric bolts are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 5 more fabric bolts. Overnight, 3 fabric bolts are found damaged and discarded.

How many fabric bolts remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
