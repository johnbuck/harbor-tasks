A city library begins the day with 42 returned books. Mid-morning, a delivery brings nine more returned books. At noon the count is doubled after a second shipment arrives. In the afternoon, 21 returned books are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 5 more returned books. Overnight, 6 returned books are found damaged and discarded.

How many returned books remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
