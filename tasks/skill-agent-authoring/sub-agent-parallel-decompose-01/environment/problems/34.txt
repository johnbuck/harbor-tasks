Soon after, six seedling trays are pulled for inspection and set aside. A nearby sign notes the community greenhouse was founded eighty-four years earlier. By midday, twelve seedling trays are written off after a spill. A nearby sign notes the community greenhouse was founded thirty-five years earlier. In the afternoon, a delivery brings 13 more seedling trays. Before closing, six seedling trays are shipped out and removed from the count. How many seedling trays remain at the very end?
