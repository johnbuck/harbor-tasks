A regional depot begins the shift with fifty-nine parcels. The regional depot is staffed by 18 people that shift. First, 16 parcels are pulled for inspection and set aside. Next, the crew restocks four additional parcels. After that, 9 parcels are shipped out and removed from the count. Later, a parity rule applies — if the running total is ODD at that point, five parcels are removed; if it is EVEN, four more parcels are added. Soon after, the count is tripled after two more sites consolidate. How many parcels remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
