A toy factory begins the day with 27 wooden trains. Mid-morning, a delivery brings twelve more wooden trains. At noon the count is tripled after two more shipments arrive. In the afternoon, 6 wooden trains are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 5 more wooden trains. Overnight, 9 wooden trains are found damaged and discarded.

How many wooden trains remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
