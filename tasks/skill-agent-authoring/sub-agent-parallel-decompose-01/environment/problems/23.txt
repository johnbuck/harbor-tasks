A toy factory begins the day with 62 wooden trains. Mid-morning, a delivery brings twelve more wooden trains. At noon the count is tripled after two more shipments arrive. In the afternoon, 6 wooden trains are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an even number, the supervisor removes exactly half of them. Overnight, 9 wooden trains are found damaged and discarded.

How many wooden trains remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
