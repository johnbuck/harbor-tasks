A city library begins the morning with forty-nine returned books. First, a capacity check applies — if the running total is GREATER THAN 48, 12 returned books are removed; OTHERWISE 7 more returned books are added. Background: eighty-seven visitors toured the city library, unrelated to the inventory. Next, 3 returned books arrive from a partner site. After that, a late shipment adds seven returned books. Later, an order ships, taking 14 returned books away. Soon after, 4 returned books are shipped out and removed from the count. By midday, a merger doubles the count. How many returned books remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
