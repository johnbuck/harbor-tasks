A bakery begins the day with 52 sourdough loaves. Mid-morning, a delivery brings three more sourdough loaves. At noon the count is doubled after a second shipment arrives. In the afternoon, 13 sourdough loaves are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 3 more sourdough loaves. Overnight, 4 sourdough loaves are found damaged and discarded.

How many sourdough loaves remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
