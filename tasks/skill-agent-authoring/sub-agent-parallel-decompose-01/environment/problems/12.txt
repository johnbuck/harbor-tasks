In the afternoon, 11 seedlings are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 1 more seedlings. Overnight, 8 seedlings are found damaged and discarded.

How many seedlings remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
