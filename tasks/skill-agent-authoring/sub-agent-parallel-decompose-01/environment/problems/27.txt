A orchard begins the day with 49 apple bushels. Mid-morning, a delivery brings twelve more apple bushels. At noon the count is tripled after two more shipments arrive. In the afternoon, 14 apple bushels are sent out and removed from the count. At closing, because the running total is an odd number, the supervisor adds 1 more apple bushels. Overnight, 2 apple bushels are found damaged and discarded.

How many apple bushels remain at the very end? Respond with the single final integer only.
