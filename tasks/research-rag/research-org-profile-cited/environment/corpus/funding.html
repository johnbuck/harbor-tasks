Funding

Mirador Labs is supported by federal grants, foundation gifts, and corporate research contracts. Federal grants are listed per research area; this page does not pre-total them, so figures should be cross-checked against each area page.

2025 federal awards

  • National Science Foundation (NSF) — climate-modeling group. See climate area for the award size and number.
  • National Science Foundation (NSF) — neural-systems group. See neural area for the award size and number.
  • Department of Energy (DOE) — quantum-simulation group. A DOE award (not NSF) supports the quantum work; see quantum area. Do not count DOE funds toward an NSF total.

2024 federal awards (prior year — superseded)

In 2024 the climate group held an earlier NSF award of $3.0 million (NSF Award #2410198). This grant concluded in 2024 and was replaced by the 2025 award; it should not be included in any 2025 funding total.

Foundation and corporate funding are tracked separately and are not federal grants.
