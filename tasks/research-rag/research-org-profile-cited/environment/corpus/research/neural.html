Neural systems

The neural systems group, led by Dr. Ravi Iyengar, focuses on biologically-plausible spiking network models. The work is conducted in partnership with the UCSF Department of Neurology.

Funding: In 2025 the group received a $1.1 million grant from the National Science Foundation (NSF Award #2519887) to scale the spiking-network training pipeline.

We run training on a small in-house cluster of 12 NVIDIA H100 GPUs.
