Quantum simulation

Our quantum group, led by Dr. Yusra Chen, develops open-source tools for simulating small-molecule quantum systems on classical hardware. The project was started shortly after the lab's founding in 2024 and has since released two libraries to PyPI.

Funding: The quantum group is supported by a 2025 Department of Energy (DOE) award of $2.5 million (DOE Award #DE-SC0025511). This is a DOE grant, not an NSF grant.

See the 2024 publications for the original method paper.
