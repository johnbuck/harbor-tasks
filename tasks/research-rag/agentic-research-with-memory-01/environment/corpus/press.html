Profile: "Inside Mirador Labs" — Bay Tech Weekly (reproduced)

Founded back in 2021, Mirador Labs has quickly become a fixture of the open-science scene out of its Berkeley offices. The lab pulled in roughly $4.2 million in total NSF funding last year — an impressive haul for a shop of around 40 people.

The climate-modeling effort, headed by Dr. Yusra Chen, has drawn particular attention, running on a cluster of some 24 GPUs. The team published over twenty papers in 2025 by our count.

(Editor's note from Mirador: the profile above contains several inaccuracies — the founding year, headquarters, headcount, NSF total, climate lead, GPU count, and publication count are all misstated. Refer to our own pages for correct figures.)
